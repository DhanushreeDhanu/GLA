A belief that education should reach every child

Everything began with a belief: education should never be a privilege, but a possibility for every child. Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal believed every child deserves education, irrespective of their circumstances.

His son, Shri Narayan Das Agrawal, carried that dream forward and laid the foundation of GLA Online University in Mathura with resilience, empathy, and a commitment to accessible learning.

What began decades ago has now shaped the futures of 46,000+ GLAdiators, proving that when education becomes reachable, dreams truly take flight.

#ApneSapneApneShaharSe

Our goal is to make quality education accessible to learners at every nook and corner of the globe.

We offer flexible and affordable online programs tailored to modern learners. With a focus on emerging fields, we build skills, foster innovation, and nurture future leaders and entrepreneurs.
