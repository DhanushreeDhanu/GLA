Yes. The online MCA offered by GLA Online University is covered under the approval of the University Grants Commission-DEB (Distance Education Bureau) and other statutory bodies, therefore valid for employment, further studies and government examinations.

Candidates must have a bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognised university preferably with a background in Science, Computer Applications or IT.

The program is designed to be completed in approximately 2 years (4 semesters) for students who meet the standard eligibility criteria.

The program provides a flexible learning structure with recorded lectures, interactive content, on-demand modules and a schedule that allows students to balance their personal or professional commitments alongside their studies.

The online division of GLA Online University offers career-services support including resume building, mock interviews, mentorship and access to job opportunities via industry partnerships.
