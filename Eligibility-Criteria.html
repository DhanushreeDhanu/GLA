Eligibility Criteria

Academic criteria designed for learners' success.

Clear and well-defined eligibility criteria designed to support learners' success from day one.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

To be eligible for the MBA program by GLA Online, applicants must hold a bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognised university.

Final-year undergraduate students awaiting their results are also eligible to apply. This allows learners to begin their academic journey without delay and step confidently toward their professional goals.

Apply Now

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

To apply for the MCA program by GLA Online, candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree from a recognised university, preferably with a background in Science, Computer Applications or Information Technology (IT)

A basic understanding of programming or logical thinking is recommended to help navigate the curriculum efficiently. Undergraduate final-year students awaiting the results can also apply.

Apply Now

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Students applying for the BBA program by GLA Online must have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board in any stream.

Applicants from all academic backgrounds are welcome - whether from commerce, science, arts, or vocational studies. The program is ideal for those aspiring to build a career in management, business, or entrepreneurship.

Apply Now

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

To be eligible for the BCA program by GLA Online, learners must have completed 10+2 or an equivalent qualification from a recognised board in any stream.

No prior coding experience is required. While familiarity with computers is helpful. There are no specific restrictions - we teach everything from the ground up.

Apply Now

Bachelor of Commerce (Hons.)

To apply for the B.COM. (Hons.) program by GLA Online, candidates must have completed 10+2 or an equivalent qualification from any recognised board, in any stream.

The B.COM. (Hons.) program by GLA Online builds a strong foundation for jobs in banking, business, or higher studies in commerce-related fields. Learners from all academic disciplines can apply.
