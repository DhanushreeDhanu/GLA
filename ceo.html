Everything began with a belief - education should never be a privilege, but a possibility for every child.

A belief held by the Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal - that every child deserves an education, irrespective of their circumstances.

Holding on to that purpose, he laid the foundation of GLA Online University in Mathura, built with resilience, empathy, and commitment.

What began decades ago has now shaped the futures of 46,000+ GLAdiators.

Vision

To make higher education globally accessible and affordable for every learner through industry-aligned, job-oriented online learning that enhances employability and prepares future-ready professionals.

Mission

  • To provide high-quality online education at an affordable cost.
  • To make online education accessible beyond geographical, economic, and temporal boundaries.
  • To offer flexible learning pathways with learner-centric schedules and adaptable academic structures.
  • To enhance employability through industry-aligned, job-oriented curricula that build future-ready skills.
