OVERVIEW

MBA by GLA Online is designed for aspiring business leaders and working professionals who seek flexibility without compromising on quality.

With a rigorous, industry-relevant curriculum, live faculty-led sessions, and a strong focus on real-world outcomes, the program empowers learners with strategic, analytical, and leadership skills. Choose from specializations in Banking & Financial Services, Business Analytics, Supply Chain Management, Operations Management, Finance Management, Human Resources Management, Information Technology Management, and Marketing Management.

Candidates must have earned a bachelor’s degree from a recognised university in any discipline to be eligible for the Online MBA at GLA Online University.

The Online MBA typically spans two years, organised in four semesters, enabling structured progression of study.

The program offers specializations such as Marketing Management, Finance Management, Human Resource Management, Supply Chain Management, Business Analytics, Information Technology Management, Operations Management, and Banking and Financial Services.

The program is delivered via online lectures, interactive learning modules, case-studies and assignments; students can access content remotely, allowing them to balance professional and personal commitments in a flexible manner.

The university offers career support services including industry-relevant specializations, placement guidance, mentorship, and networking opportunities through its online programs.

Graduates can pursue roles such as Marketing Manager, Financial Analyst, HR Manager, Operations Manager, Business Development Manager, and may also go for higher studies or specialised certifications.
